What was Jerry Lucas known for?

Jerry Lucas, one of the best scoring and rebounding forwards ever, was named to the All-Star team seven times and won the 1965 All-Star Game MVP. At Ohio State University, Lucas played center on a legendary Buckeyes team that went 78-6, won three Big Ten titles and captured the NCAA Championship in 1960.
