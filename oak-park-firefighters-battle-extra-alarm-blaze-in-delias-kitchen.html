Oak Park firefighters battle extra-alarm blaze in Delia's Kitchen

Publish date: 2024-03-16
OAK PARK, Ill. (WLS) — Firefighters in Oak Park are battling an extra-alarm fire Tuesday morning.

The fire is in a three-story building with Delia’s Restaurant on the first floor in the 1000-block of West Lake Street. Fire officials said the restaurant sustained heavy water damage.

Chopper 7HD flew over the scene as heavy smoke billowed from the building as firefighters sprayed water on the flames coming from the roof.

There are no reports of any injuries. Lake Street will be closed in the area for several hours until the scene is cleared.
