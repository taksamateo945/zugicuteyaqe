Kim Kardashian Shows Off Pre-Pregnancy Curves in Skintight Latex

Publish date: 2024-07-19

Summer may officially be over, but Kim Kardashian is keeping things hot! The reality star shared several steamy photos of herself, courtesy of KimKardashianWest.com and shot by Steven Gomillion for Kim Kardashian West, clad in skintight latex outfits on Instagram on Wednesday, Sept. 23 — all in the name of business.

“Love this shoot I did with @stevengomillion for my app & website,” she captioned a photo of herself — before her pregnancy curves appeared — in a sexy black two-piece latex bodysuit that shows off her ample assets and tiny waist. In the shot, Kardashian, 34, is leaning her head back under an outdoor shower with her eyes closed.

In another photo the mother-to-be posted, she sizzles in a deep-cut, hot pink dress that hugs her curves as she lounges by a pool with her head tilted toward the sun.

Kardashian and her sisters Kourtney, Khloe, Kendall, and Kylie, launched individualized apps earlier this month, each focusing on the Kardashian-Jenners’ different interests.

Kim’s will feature fashion, beauty tutorials, and selfies; Kourtney’s will hone in on motherhood; Khloe’s app will follow her fitness progress; Kendall’s program will feature her adventures as a model; and Kylie’s app will spotlight makeup, her new home, and her dog, Normie Jenner.

“Yes, we are sisters,” Kim said at a tech talk at the Apple Store in NYC’s Soho neighborhood on Monday, Sept. 14. “But our passions are very much individual.”

Immediately following the launch of their apps, it became apparent that Kylie was not only the biggest seller of the five, but also ranked No. 1 among all free apps.
