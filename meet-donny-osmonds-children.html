Meet Donny Osmonds Children

Publish date: 2024-07-13

Donny Osmond’s children are five in number, and their names are Donald Clark Osmond Jr., Jeremy James Osmond, Brandon Michael Osmond, Christopher Glenn Osmond, and Joshua Davis Osmond. He had all his children with Debra Osmond, whom he married on May 8, 1978, and has remained committed to her since then.

The American singer, actor, and television personality Donny Osmond is well-known and celebrated in the entertainment industry. Throughout his brilliant and lengthy career, which has lasted more than five decades, he has garnered countless honors and prizes. But Donny Osmond is also a proud parent in addition to being a well-known celebrity. This article will cover everything there is to know about Donny Osmond’s children.

Donny Osmond’s Children are all from Debbie Osmond

Donny Osmon had all his five children with his wife, Debbie Osmond. Since their union in 1978, Donny and Debbie Osmond have been together for 45 years now. All of Donny Osmond’s children have achieved success in their own right as adults. Despite coming from a talented family, they each took a different course in life. Donny continues to be a devoted and encouraging parent to all his kids and is proud of them all.

Donald Clark Osmond Jr. (Don Jr.) Is Donny Osmond’s First Child 

  • Date of Birth: July 31, 1979
  • Age: 44 years old
  • Mother: Debbie Osmond
  • Occupation: Business Executive 

Don Jr. is the oldest child of Donny Osmond and his wife, Debbie Osmond. He was born on July 31, 1979, and is now 44 years old in 2023. Don Jr. decided to follow in his father’s footsteps; he works in the entertainment industry as a marketing executive.

As a business executive, he is a managing partner with OzComm Marketing, which has his father as one of its numerous high-profile clients. Aside from his corporate work, Don Jr. has also made several appearances on his father’s television shows and has worked as a producer.

On a personal note, Donald is noted to have had it rough with the press while growing up, and in fact, it informed his decision to steer clear of pursuing a career as an entertainer. Regardless, he is still in one or several ways involved in the industry as a marketing expert.

About his family, Don Jr. is married and has children. He married his heartthrob, a certain Jessica Nelson, sometime in 2010, and they are now parents to four children. Their youngest child was born in the year 2020, and it totaled Donny Osmond’s grandchildren to twelve.

Jeremy James Osmond Is Donny Osmond’s Second Child, Born In 1981

  • Date of Birth: June 8, 1981
  • Age: 42 years old
  • Mother: Debbie Osmond
  • Occupation: Physical Therapist 

Jeremy Osmond was born to Donny and Debbie Osmond on June 8, 1981. And as their second son, he is currently 42 years old in 2023. While growing up, Jeremy went through a terrible rebellious phase as a teen. He had a thing for piercing at that time and did get his tongue and eyebrow pierced, much to the dismay and disapproval of his parents.

Jeremy didn’t stop there as he went on to dye his hair with leopard spots. At the height of his rebellion, he left his family’s Mormon church and remained incognito for four years. However, with time, he got over his rebellious ways, reconnected with his family, and went on a church mission to Italy in 2001. Donny Osmond described Jeremy’s rebellion in his teens as his way of showing his disapproval with all the weight that comes with being an Osmond.

Jeremy is not only successful in his career, he is also a happy family man. The physical therapist married his wife, Melissa Osmond, in 2002, and they are parents to three lovely children.

Their first child, a son named Dylan James, was born in 2005. He was followed by Ryder James, their second son, who was born in 2013, while the youngest child in their family, a daughter named Emery James, was born in 2008. James’ children add to Donny Osmond’s twelve grandchildren, and his daughter Emery is his only granddaughter so far.

Jeremy James Osmond is a Physical Therapist

Having desired a life very different from his father’s, Jeremy is pursuing a career as a physical therapist. He currently works as an executive director at Pointe Meadows Health and Rehabilitation, which is situated in Lehi, Utah, United States of America.

Before his current job at Pointe Meadows Health and Rehabilitation, Jeremy James Osmond earned his bachelor’s degree (2008-2011) in Exercise and Sports Science and his doctorate in Physical Therapy, both from the University of Utah.

He then worked at Orchard Park Post-Acute Rehab as director of rehab, RED Enterprises of Utah, LLC as its CEO, Osmond Physical Therapy as President/CEO, and Provo Nursing and Rehab as Director of Rehab.

What Happened to Donny Osmond’s Son with The Police?

Jeremy Osmond was arrested on July 21, 2012, for unreasonably firing a fireman in Cedar Hills, Utah. He, however, claimed he was only target shooting in a deserted area in the Wasatch Mountains, Utah-Idaho border before the police pulled up and gave him a citation. He claimed he only had to visit the police station to submit his fingerprint and was not actually taken in a police car. He also revealed that they allowed him to keep his gun but was fined $200.
