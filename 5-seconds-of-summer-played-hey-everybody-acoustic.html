5 Seconds of Summer played Hey Everybody acoustic

Publish date: 2024-07-31

Yesterday 5 Seconds of Summer were on Sunday Brunch and they played an acoustic version of their new single Hey Everybody. Luke and his note changes, Michael’s rough voice, Calum’s cute faces and Ashton’s surprising talents in back vocals are up. The boys are flawless as usual and we all love it! Guitars and moves in synchronisation and amazing vocals, it’s a video you must watch now! Hey Everybody acoustic is all you need to be happy right now. Who knows, we might have a studio version of this sooner or later.
