Flipkart Grid 5.0 Coding Challenge: How to Join and Win Prizes

Publish date: 2024-01-29

Flipkart, one of the leading e-commerce platforms in India, has launched the fifth edition of its coding challenge, Flipkart Grid 5.0 Software Development.

The challenge is open to students and professionals who are passionate about solving real-world problems using technology.

Here is everything you need to know about Flipkart Grid 5.0 Software Development and how to participate and win prizes.

Flipkart Grid 5.0 Challenge

Flipkart Grid 5.0 Software Development is a coding challenge that aims to test the participants’ skills and creativity in developing software solutions for various domains such as e-commerce, logistics, payments, and social media.

The challenge consists of three rounds:

Round 1: Online Coding Test

The participants have to solve two coding problems within 90 minutes using any programming language of their choice.

The test will be conducted on HackerEarth platform and the participants will be evaluated based on their accuracy and efficiency.

Round 2: Online Hackathon

Shortlisted participants from Round 1 will be given a problem statement based on one of the domains mentioned above.

They will have to develop a software solution within 48 hours using any technology stack of their choice.

The solution will be judged based on its functionality, scalability, usability, and innovation.

Round 3: Grand Finale

Top teams from Round 2 will be invited to present their solutions to a panel of experts from Flipkart and other industry leaders.

The teams will have to demonstrate their solutions live and answer questions from the judges.

The final winners will be decided based on their performance and presentation.

Flipkart Grid 5.0 Prizes

The Flipkart Grid 5.0 Software Development offers attractive prizes for the participants who excel in the challenge.

  • First Prize: INR 3 lakhs
  • Second Prize: INR 2 lakhs
  • Third Prize: INR 1 lakh
  • Best All-Women Team: INR 50,000
  • Best Campus Team: INR 50,000
  • Best Professional Team: INR 50,000
  • Best Solution for Each Domain: INR 25,000
  • Participation Certificates for All Participants

Flipkart Grid 5.0 Registration

Flipkart Grid 5.0 Software Development is open for registration.

Participants can register as individuals or as teams of up to four members.

The registration is free and can be done online on the official website of Flipkart Grid.

Flipkart Grid 5.0 Software Development is a great opportunity for students and professionals who are interested in software development and want to showcase their talent and skills.

The challenge also provides a platform for learning, networking, and exposure to the latest trends and technologies in the industry.

If you are one of them, don’t miss this chance and register now.
