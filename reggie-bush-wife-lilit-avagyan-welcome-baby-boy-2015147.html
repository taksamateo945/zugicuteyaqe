Reggie Bush, Wife Lilit Avagyan Welcome Baby Boy

Publish date: 2024-08-09

His little footballer! Reggie Bush and his wife, Lilit Avagyan, have welcomed their second child together, a baby boy, Us Weekly can confirm.

Us confirmed in April that the San Francisco 49ers running back, 30, and Avagyan, 27, were expanding their family. The couple are already parents to 2-year-old daughter Briseis.
