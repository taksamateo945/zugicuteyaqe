Teresa Giudice Heres How She Feels About Getting Married Again Following Louie Ruelas Reve

Publish date: 2024-05-06

Is Teresa Giudice ready to get married again while dating Louie Ruelas? After all, the man made it very clear that he’s thinking of popping the big question.

With that being said, would ‘The Real Housewives of New Jersey’ star accept the proposal when it comes her way?

There is no doubt that Teresa and Louie love each other very much and are really happy together so the man told TMZ not too long ago that he wants to make it forever by getting married to Teresa!

While she was by his side at the time, she remained more reserved when it comes to her thoughts on marriage which might make it seem like she’s not willing to make this step again after her Joe Giudice divorce.

But, one insider tells HollywoodLife that couldn’t be farther from the truth.

‘Teresa feels like this is it for her when it comes to her relationship with Louie. She has honestly never been happier and is in the happiest, healthiest relationship she’s ever been in. She could see herself marrying Louie, absolutely! The kids love him and he is very good to her kids.’

Another person who would not be surprised if Teresa and Louie tie the knot is her sister-in-law, Melissa Gorga, who previously told the same news outlet that ‘I just do not think he is a fling. I think that they are very serious about each other and they love each other very much. I could tell if he was just a fling and he does not feel like a fling.’

Furthermore, another RHONJ star who would love to see the couple walk down the aisle is Dolores Catania, who raved about them while on HollywoodLife TVTalk.
