Little Women Star Terra Jole Marries Joe Gnoffo in &quot;Magical&quot; Ceremony

Publish date: 2024-04-07

Making it official! On Sunday, June 21, Terra Jole and Joe Gnoffo finally tied the knot in front of friends and family, Us Weekly can exclusively confirm. 

The Little Women: Terra's Little Family stars have been waiting for this day for a long time after welcoming daughter Penny in March. 

"Today was magical," Jole tells Us Weekly of her nuptials. "Walking down the aisle to the man of my dreams and to finally be able to call Joe my husband — what more can a girl ask for?"
