Stefania LaVie Owen Bio & Family: Age, Parents, Siblings, Height

Her Career & Earnings: TV Shows, Movies, Net Worth

Stefania’s other recognizable role came as Dorrit Bradshaw in the teen comedy-drama series titled The Carrie Diaries, from 2014 to 2014. Following that, she appeared in several movies and TV series including Kampus (2015), All We Had (2016), Chance (2016-2017), and The Beach Bum (2019).
