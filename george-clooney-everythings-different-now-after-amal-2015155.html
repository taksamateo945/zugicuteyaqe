George Clooney: &quot;Everything's Different Now&quot; After Amal

Publish date: 2024-02-18

First comes love, then comes marriage! Everything is awesome for George Clooney after marrying Amal Alamuddin. The once-eternal bachelor sat down with George Stephanopoulos on Good Morning America on Thursday, May 14, where he discussed the latest updates on his marriage.

"Everything’s different now," Clooney said. "Actually it is. I was going to make a joke but it is actually."

The Tomorrowland actor, 54, and international human rights lawyer, 37, married last September in a lavish ceremony before their famous friends and family members in Venice, Italy.

"I couldn’t be happier," Clooney gushed on GMA. "We’re having a really fun time. We’re working on fixing up a house in England that we’ll be spending a lot of time in just outside of London. And we’ve been sort of traveling the world. We’re on our way to Shanghai."
