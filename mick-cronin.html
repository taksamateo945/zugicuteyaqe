Who is Mick Cronin:

Mick Cronin is a famous Basketball Coach. He was born on July 17, 1971 and his birthplace is Cincinnati, OH. Mick is also well known as, Basketball coach known for being at the helm of the Cincinnati Bearcats, a position he took over in 2006. He’d previously been the head coach of Murray State University.

Mick is originated from United States. He spent three years as an assistant coach to Rick Pitino in the early 2000s.
